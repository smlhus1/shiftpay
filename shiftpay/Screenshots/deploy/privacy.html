All data stays on your device.

Last updated: March 1, 2026

The fundamental principle: All personal data stays on your device. Period. No accounts, no cloud storage, no analytics, no tracking. The only network call (OCR) is stateless and stores nothing.

About the timesheet photo

Honest assessment

The one privacy consideration is the timesheet photo sent for OCR processing. This image may contain personal information (names, employee IDs on the schedule). Here is exactly what happens:

  • When you take a photo of your timesheet, the image is sent over HTTPS to a Supabase Edge Function.
  • The Edge Function forwards the image to Anthropic's Claude Vision API for text extraction.
  • Anthropic's API processes the image in memory and does not store input data (per their data policy).
  • Our Edge Function does not log, cache, or store the image or any extracted data.
  • The structured shift data (dates, times, shift types) is returned to your device and saved locally.
  • The original image is never stored anywhere — not on our servers, not in any database.

What we don't do

  • No account: We don't collect your name, email, phone number, or any identifier.
  • No analytics: We don't use Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, or any tracking SDK.
  • No ads: We don't show advertisements or share data with ad networks.
  • No telemetry: We don't track app usage, screen views, or feature adoption.
  • No cloud sync: Your data is not uploaded to any cloud service.
  • No crash reporting: We don't use Sentry, Crashlytics, or similar services.

Your rights

Since all your data is stored locally on your device:

  • Access: All your data is visible within the app at all times.
  • Deletion: Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all data. There is no server-side data to request deletion of.
  • Portability: PDF export of shift history is planned for a future update.

Children's privacy

ShiftPay is designed for working adults. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16. Since we collect no personal data at all, this is not applicable in practice.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Since we don't have your email or any contact information, we cannot notify you directly — please check this page periodically.
