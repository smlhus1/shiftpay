## Description (max 4000 characters)

```
You work the shifts. You deserve to know if you're paid correctly.

If you work shifts in healthcare, retail, hospitality, or any job with evening, night, weekend, and holiday supplements — you know the problem. Your payslip is a wall of numbers. The systems are closed. Your history disappears after a month. And when something looks off, you have no way to check.

ShiftPay is a verification tool for shift workers.

HOW IT WORKS

1. Import your timesheet
Snap a photo of your printed schedule — ShiftPay reads it automatically. You can also import from a CSV file, or add shifts manually.

2. Set your pay rates
Enter your base hourly rate and any supplements: evening, night, weekend, holiday, overtime. Your rates, your numbers.

3. See what you should earn
ShiftPay calculates your expected pay for any period based on the rates you entered. Compare it against your actual payslip. If the numbers don't match, now you have a starting point for a conversation with your employer.

FEATURES

• Photo import: Take a photo of your timesheet, ShiftPay extracts your shifts
• CSV import: Upload schedule files from your employer
• Manual entry: Add or edit shifts by hand
• Custom pay rates: Base rate + evening, night, weekend, holiday, and overtime supplements
• Shift confirmation: Track whether you actually worked each shift
• Overtime tracking: Log extra hours with a few taps
• Monthly summaries: Planned vs. actual hours and expected pay at a glance
• Export: Download your data as CSV for your own records
• Multi-language: Available in English, Norwegian, Swedish, and Danish

BUILT FOR PRIVACY

ShiftPay stores everything on your device. No account needed. No cloud. No data collection. Your shifts, your rates, your pay — it stays on your phone.

The only time data leaves your device is when you use the photo import feature. The image is sent to a secure server for text recognition, processed immediately, and not stored.

WHO IS THIS FOR?

Shift workers who want to verify their pay calculations. Nurses, care workers, retail staff, hotel employees, warehouse workers — anyone whose payslip includes complex supplements and who wants a simple way to double-check the math.

ShiftPay is a verification tool, not a legal or financial advice service. The numbers it shows are based on the rates you enter — accuracy depends on those rates matching your actual employment agreement.

You shouldn't need a spreadsheet to know if you're paid right.
```

_~2 250 tegn. Trygt under 4 000-grensa. Språket er tilpasset App Review-paranoia: "verification tool", "starting point for a conversation", eksplisitt disclaimer at appen ikke gir rådgivning._
