## Full description (max 4000 characters)

**Are you getting paid correctly?**

If you work shifts — in healthcare, retail, hospitality, or any field with variable hours — you know the problem. Complex pay rules, evening and night supplements, overtime, and weekend rates make it nearly impossible to verify your payslip. Most payroll systems are closed, and you can barely see last month's data.

ShiftPay puts you back in control.

**How it works:**
1. Import your timesheet — snap a photo, pick a CSV file, or add shifts manually
2. Set your pay rates — base rate plus evening, night, weekend, holiday, and overtime supplements
3. See your expected pay — calculated automatically based on your confirmed shifts
4. Compare with your payslip — enter what you actually received and see the difference

**Key features:**

- Photo import with AI-powered OCR — just photograph your printed schedule
- CSV import for digital timesheets
- Manual shift entry for one-off extras
- Configurable tariff rates with all common supplements
- Monthly summary with planned vs. actual hours
- Pay comparison — expected vs. actual, with clear over/underpaid indicator
- Shift confirmation — mark shifts as completed, missed, or overtime
- Dual pay periods — separate tracking for regular and extra shifts
- Export shifts as CSV for your own records
- Light and dark theme
- Available in English, Norwegian, Swedish, and Danish

**Privacy first:**
All your data stays on your device. No account required. No cloud storage. No tracking. The only network call is the optional photo OCR, which processes your image and returns the result without storing anything.

ShiftPay is built by a shift worker's partner who saw the problem firsthand. It's the tool we wished existed.
